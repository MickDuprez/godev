Software Development and Consultants

With over 18 years of experience in both Desktop and Web development we can supply you with the services you need to make your next project a reality.

Web Sites and Web Applications

We specialise in building custom database Web Applications that are fast, customised to work the same way you do and will scale with your business.

We can also provide a full range of services from setting up your host and installing a simple ‘brochure’ site, WordPress or Drupal CMS sites or we can build a custom site from scratch using the latest technologies depending on your requirements.

Desktop/CAD

We specialise in the customisation of applications such as MS Office and CAD applications such as BricsCAD, AutoCAD and Tekla Structures among others or we can build complete database backed GUI applications from scratch.
